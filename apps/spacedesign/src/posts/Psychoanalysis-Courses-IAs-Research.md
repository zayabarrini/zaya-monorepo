In the first moment of working with the imaginary that we can be with writing to the first three seminars always articulating imaginary with the real symbolic a second moment that begins around seminar 4 in which he will work primarily with the symbolic this will continue until at least seminar 8 always articulating if although equal imaginary to the real, from seminar 9 he will give a lot of emphasis He will dedicate himself a lot to defining the real, until seminar 20, always articulating the real with the symbolic and imaginary, and from seminar 21 onwards he will say that he has finally found a way to talk about all three at the same time. - Michele Roman Faria
