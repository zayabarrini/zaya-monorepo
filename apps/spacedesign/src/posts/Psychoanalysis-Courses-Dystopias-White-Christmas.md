### Joe, Beth - Joe decides to tell why he came to the outpost and explains that he lived with Beth. One...

Joe, Beth - Joe decides to tell why he came to the outpost and explains that he lived with Beth. One night they have dinner with their friends Tim and Gita and Beth is feeling tired and absent. She goes to bed and Joe cleans the kitchen. However, he accidentally drops the trash on the floor and sees a positive pregnancy test. He questions Beth and she says she is going to abort the baby. They argue and Beth blocks him and leaves. One day, he accidentally finds her silhouette on the street and discovers that she is raising her son with his father. However, he cannot see either Beth or the child as he is still blocked. But when Beth dies in a train accident, Joe goes to her father's house to see his son and gets a surprise.
