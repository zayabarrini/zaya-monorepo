### Teaching, Knowledge, Enlightenment

We imagine that Lacan knows. That he had a successful clinic. That he was courageous in putting himself in the role of teaching. Teaching and knowing. Lacan understood Freud, while others did not understand him so well. Lacan constitutes himself as an Ideal for the Psychoanalyst.

For knowing, knowing-how, teaching. Knowing as much as Lacan. Dialoguing with Lacan. Explaining Lacan. Teaching about Psychoanalysis. Clarity of Ideas. Enlightenment. Dialectics of Enlightenment.
