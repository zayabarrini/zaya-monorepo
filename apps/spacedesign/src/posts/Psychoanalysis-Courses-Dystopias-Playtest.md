He's a twenty-something world traveler with a scruffy beard and backpack, looking for a break from his annoying mother after his father recently passed away from Alzheimer's. He travels the world, partying in various countries Instagram-style, but when he arrives in London, his identity is stolen and his money is drained from his accounts. Cooper arrives to find himself in a veritable maze of a house, where guests interact with devices rather than each other. While the spaces he walks through are warm and somewhat inviting, when he arrives at the room he will be working in, it is cold and sterile. He then learns about his assignment: in true Freudian style, they will immerse him in a video game where he will face his own fears through an implant in the back of his neck.
