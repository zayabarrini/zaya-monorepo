# Admission Conditions and Affirmative Action Policies...

Admission Conditions and Affirmative Action Policies

The Forum's Scholarship and Grants Committee is working on formulating an Affirmative Action Policies Project to democratize access and training of new analysts, aimed at low-income professionals, primarily Afro-descendants and indigenous peoples. The selection criteria will be subject to specific internal regulations and will follow specific guidelines through a public notice, formulated by this Committee. The objective is to implement an admission policy for training that supports the essential proportionality of the Forum's members and participants with the ethnic composition of the Brazilian population.
