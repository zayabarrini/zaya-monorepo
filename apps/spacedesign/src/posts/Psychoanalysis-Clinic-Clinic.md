# Psychoanalytic Clinic

# Psychoanalyst Zaya Barrini

Zaya Barrini is a clinical psychoanalyst, supervisor, computer engineer from the Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica (ITA), scriptwriter, producer and film director. She works at the interface between psychoanalysis, cinema and languages, producing clinical and theoretical courses, films, texts and scripts, participating in and organizing lectures, study groups and research in these areas. She is a researcher in the area of ​​Artificial Intelligence, psychoanalysis, logic, philosophy of language through the development of algorithms, schemes and graphs based on dialogues with the works of Freud, Lacan, Judith Butler and Hegel, among others.

Experience with issues in various job markets: Public exams, Armed Forces, Entrepreneurship, Universities, Sexualities, Migration, Class and Race. Dimension of the Said and construction of fantasy.
