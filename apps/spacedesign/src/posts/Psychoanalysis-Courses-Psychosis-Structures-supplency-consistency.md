### Loss and Restitution

The neurotic strategy of inscribing enjoyment, both in obsessive neurosis and in hysteria, would be marked by the equivalence between loss and restitution. The less enjoyment, imposed by castration, would be made equivalent to an additional enjoyment, understood by the phallic formations of restitution, such as the symptom. The neurotic presupposition is that lesss and more are commensurable and reversible and therefore the lack in the Other is identified with the demand in the subject.
