## 🏛️ Our Vision: A Space for Creation & Connection

We are building a physical and digital **Psychoanalysis, Digital Art, and Cinema Space** to foster:

- **Social bonds** and meaningful exchange
- **Artistic expression** and interdisciplinary dialogue
- **Clinical training**, seminars, and research
- **Public events** like _SextArte_
- A hub to **work, elaborate, speak, write, and record**

### **Our Structure**

- **Psychoanalysis:** Clinical practice, supervision, case studies, seminars, cartels, and language study.
- **Cinema:** An international studio integrating film with psychoanalytic and artistic exploration.
- **Digital Wellbeing:** Developing an OS that blends psychoanalysis with AI.
