#### **I. Psychoanalysis & Psychosis**

_Focus: Clinical and theoretical works on psychosis, madness, and Lacanian theory._

1.  **Lacan on Madness: Madness, Madness, You're Not Making Sense** (Book)
    - _Contents Include:_ The case of the baby diaper man; From psychotic illness to psychotic existence; The suicide bomber; "You cannot choose to go crazy"; Psychotic transference; Melancholia; Narcissistic neurosis; Madness and creation in Woolf and Joyce; Schizophrenic writing.

2.  **Conversations psychanalytiques avec des psychotiques ordinaires et extraordinaires** (Book - French)
    - _Contents Include:_ Carence du fantasme fondamental; Syndrome d’Asperger ou psychose ordinaire?; Meurtre immotivé; Parricide; Spécificité du fantasme nécrophile; Phénomène psychosomatique; Fonctions de la lettre et de l’écrit; Le fourre-tout des borderlines.

3.  **Repères pour la psychose ordinaire** by Jean-Claude Maleval (Book - French)
    - _Contents Include:_ Appréhension de la psychose ordinaire; Modes de stabilisation; Ratages du nouage borroméen; Inconsistance du sujet; Glissements imaginaires et troubles de l'identité; Signes discrets de nouages non borroméens; Fantasmes pris à une image indélébile; Sinthomes dans la psychose ordinaire; Du fantasme de changement de sexe au sinthome transsexuel.

---

#### **II. Gender, Sexuality & Transgender Studies**

_Focus: Theoretical explorations of gender, sexuality, and transness through a psychoanalytic lens._

1.  **Psychoanalysis, Gender, and Sexualities: From Feminism to Trans** (Book)
    - _Contents Include:_ Freud’s ménage à quatre; The gender question from Freud to Lacan; Undoing the interpellation of gender; Queering psychoanalysis; Oscar Wilde: Father and Son; The anthropology of kinship; Tiresias and the Other Sexual Difference; Scenes of Self-conduct in contemporary Iran; Becoming Being: Chance, Choice and the Troubles of Trans\*cursivity; Transgender Quarrels.

2.  **Transgender Psychoanalysis: A Lacanian Perspective on Trans Identities** (Book)
    - _Contents Include:_ Depathologizing Trans; Gender in the Blender; Strange Bedfellows: Psychoanalysis and Sexology; A Natural Experiment; From Trance to Trans; Simulation, Expression, and Truth; The Singular Universality of Trans; Plastic Sex; Freud's Scatalog; Clinic of the Clinamen; Making Life Livable.

3.  **Pour un regard neuf de la psychanalyse sur le genre et les parentalités** (Book - French)
    - _Contents Include:_ La psychanalyse n’est pas une morale; Fondamentaux de la pulsion; Remarques sur lesdites « minorités sexuelles »; Que fait l’homoparentalité à la psychanalyse ?; Œdipe Now; Questions d’avenir pour la psychanalyse; Genre, classe, « race » et subalternité.
