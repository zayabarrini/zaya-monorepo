Note on Russian Verb Structure

Russian verbs typically consist of: Prefix (приставка) + Root (корень) + Suffix (суффикс) + Ending (окончание).

Prefixes often change the meaning of the verb (like "за-" for beginning an action, "пере-" for repeating). Suffixes often indicate aspect (perfective/imperfective) or other grammatical features. Endings change according to person, number, gender, and tense.
