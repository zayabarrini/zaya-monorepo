# Bibliography

"Psychoanalysis in China: Contemporary Growth". 2012. by Shen Heyong and John M. R. Hughes: This book explores the development of psychoanalysis in China and its clinical applications, as well as the interactions between psychoanalytic theory and Chinese culture. "The Underground Vision". 2009. by Xiaowei Jiang: This book offers a look at psychoanalysis in China, addressing issues such as sexuality, family, and Chinese culture, using a psychoanalytic lens. "Psychoanalysis: An East-West Dialogue". 2016. by Xiaowei Jiang and Henderikus J. Stam: This book examines the dialogue between Western psychoanalysis and Chinese culture, exploring the application and adaptation of psychoanalysis in Chinese contexts. "The Wound and the Bond: An Introduction to Chinese Psychoanalysis". 2016. by Yu Xin: This book explores Chinese psychoanalysis and its challenges in understanding the Chinese psyche, focusing on issues such as family and identity.
