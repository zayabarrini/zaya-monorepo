# Fethi Benslama

Fethi Benslama is a psychoanalyst, member of the Tunisian Academy, former director of the UFR of psychoanalytic studies of the University Paris Diderot, where he created the Center for the Study of radicalization and their treatments .CERT.. His research, rooted in clinical practice, have given him in-depth knowledge of Islamist radicalization and its violent outcomes. He has notably published Psychoanalysis in the Test of Islam and A Furious Desire for Sacrifice, the Super-Muslim.

# Bibliography

"Les Arabes, leur destin et le nôtre" .The Arabs, Their Destiny and Ours. by Malek Chebel: This book is not strictly about psychoanalysis, but explores Arab culture and its interaction with the Western world, including psychological and cultural aspects. "La Grande Perturbation" .The Great Disturbance. by Fethi Benslama: In this book, Tunisian psychoanalyst Fethi Benslama explores the issues of youth and extremism in Arab society. "Psychopathologie de l'Arabie préislamique" .Psychopathology of Pre-Islamic Arabia. by Rachid Aouameur: This book examines psychopathology in pre-Islamic Arabian society. "Le temps de l'adieu: Une psychopathologie du 'déjà-vu'" .The Time of Farewell: A Psychopathology of 'I've Seen That'. by Rachid Aouameur: In this book, the author explores the notion of "déjà-vu" and its importance in psychopathology.
