# Biography

Zaya Barrini is a clinical psychoanalyst, supervisor, computer engineer from the Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica (ITA) and a professor of psychoanalysis. He works at the interface between subjectivity, sexuality and languages, producing theoretical courses, texts and videos and participating in lectures, study groups and research in these areas. He is a researcher in the areas of artificial intelligence, psychoanalysis, logic, philosophy of language through the development of algorithms, diagrams and graphs based on dialogues with the works of Freud, Lacan, Judith Butler and Hegel, among others.
