Example Sentences with Color-Coded Syntax

मैं अच्छी किताबें लिखता हूँ।
Translation: I write good books. (Literally: I good books write)
Breakdown: "मैं" (I) + "अच्छी" (good - feminine plural) + "किताबें" (books) + "लिखता हूँ" (write - 1st person singular)
वह लड़केको अच्छा उपहार देता है।
Translation: He gives a good gift to the boy.
Breakdown: "वह" (He) + "लड़के को" (to the boy - dative case) + "अच्छा" (good - masculine singular) + "उपहार" (gift) + "देता है" (gives - 3rd person singular)
हम अच्छे लड़कोंसे बात करते हैं।
Translation: We talk with good boys.
Breakdown: "हम" (We) + "अच्छे" (good - masculine plural) + "लड़कों से" (with boys - instrumental case) + "बात" (talk) + "करते हैं" (do - 1st person plural)
तुम अच्छी लड़कीका नाम लिखते हो।
Translation: You write the good girl's name.
Breakdown: "तुम" (You - informal) + "अच्छी" (good - feminine singular) + "लड़की का" (girl's - genitive case) + "नाम" (name) + "लिखते हो" (write - 2nd person informal)
