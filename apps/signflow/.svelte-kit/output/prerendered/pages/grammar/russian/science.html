Russian Spelling Rules

✍️

Basic Spelling Rules

  • After Ж, Ш, Ч, Щ, Ц, write И instead of Ы
  • After Ж, Ш, Ч, Щ, Ц, write А instead of Я
  • After Ж, Ш, Ч, Щ, Ц, write У instead of Ю
  • Write О under stress, А without stress in some roots
  • Ь soft sign palatalizes preceding consonant
🔤

Vowel Reduction

  • Unstressed О sounds like А
  • Unstressed Е sounds like И
  • Unstressed Я sounds like И
  • This is called "аканье" (akan'ye)
🔄

Consonant Assimilation

  • Voiced consonants become voiceless at word end
  • Voiceless consonants become voiced before voiced consonants
  • Г often becomes В in endings
  • Consonant clusters simplify in pronunciation

Accent & Pronunciation

In Russian, stress is crucial and can change word meaning:

Compare: за́мок (castle) vs замо́к (lock)

🎯

Stress Rules

  • Stress is unpredictable - must be memorized
  • Can fall on any syllable
  • Changes vowel pronunciation
  • Changes in different grammatical forms
  • Sometimes marked with ́ in learning materials
🗣️

Pronunciation Tips

  • Roll your Р with tongue tip
  • Ы - smile while saying "ee"
  • Щ - longer, softer "sh"
  • Palatalize consonants before soft vowels
  • Practice vowel reduction patterns

Mathematics Operators in Russian

Basic Arithmetic

+
Сложение (slo-ZHEH-nee-ye)
Addition: 2 + 3 = 5 (два плюс три равно пять)
−
Вычитание (vih-chi-TAH-nee-ye)
Subtraction: 5 − 2 = 3 (пять минус два равно три)
× или ⋅
Умножение (oom-no-ZHEH-nee-ye)
Multiplication: 3 × 4 = 12 (три умножить на четыре равно двенадцать)
÷ или /
Деление (deh-LEH-nee-ye)
Division: 12 ÷ 3 = 4 (двенадцать разделить на три равно четыре)

Physics Operators & Terms

F = ma

Второй закон Ньютона (vtah-ROY za-KON NYOO-tah-nah)

Newton's Second Law: Force equals mass times acceleration

F - сила (SEE-lah) - Force

m - масса (MAS-sah) - Mass

a - ускорение (oo-skah-REH-nee-ye) - Acceleration

E = mc²

Эквивалентность массы и энергии (eh-kvee-vah-LYENT-nast' MAS-sih ee eh-NEHR-ghee-ee)

Mass-energy equivalence

E - энергия (eh-NEHR-ghee-yah) - Energy

c - скорость света (SKO-rast' SVEH-tah) - Speed of light

∇·E = ρ/ε₀

Закон Гаусса (za-KON GOW-sah)

Gauss's Law for electricity

∇ - набла (NAH-blah) - Del operator

ρ - плотность заряда (PLOT-nast' zah-RYAH-dah) - Charge density

ħ = h/2π

Приведённая постоянная Планка (pree-vee-DYON-nah-yah pah-STOY-an-nah-yah PLAN-kah)

Reduced Planck constant

ħ - аш с чертой (ash s cher-TOY) - h-bar

Used in quantum mechanics
